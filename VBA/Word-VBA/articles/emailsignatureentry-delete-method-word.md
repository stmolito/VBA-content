# EmailSignatureEntry.Delete Method (Word)

Deletes the specified email signature.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **Delete**

 _expression_ Required. A variable that represents an **[EmailSignatureEntry](emailsignatureentry-object-word.md)** object.
